Welcome to the ultimate preparation hub for **Molecular Electronics Designer** interviews! This repository contains curated questions, answers, and study resources covering nanofabrication, quantum transport, self-assembly, and characterization.

Whether you are preparing for a job interview as a Nanotechnology Engineer, Molecular Electronics Researcher, or working on Nanoelectronics and Single-Molecule Junction designs, this comprehensive Q&A guide covers critical concepts like coherent tunneling, self-assembled monolayers (SAMs), STM/AFM characterization, and advanced photolithography techniques.
